SNAC(01,02)  CLI_READY  

This is the last snac in protocol negotiation sequence. See also known families list. Clent send it when it ready for normal function.

 00 01   word   SNAC family
 00 02   word   SNAC subtype
 00 00   word   SNAC flags
 xx xx xx xx   dword   SNAC request-id
 xx xx   word   family number #1
 xx xx   word   family #1 version
 xx xx xx xx   dword   family #1 dll version
 ...    ...    ...
 xx xx   word   family number #n
 xx xx   word   family #n version
 xx xx xx xx   dword   family #n dll version
