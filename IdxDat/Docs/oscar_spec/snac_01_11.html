SNAC(01,1F)  CLI_SETxIDLExTIME  

Client use this snac to set its idle time (in seconds). It should send this snac once when go idle and then send it again (with zero value) when go back.

 00 01   word   SNAC family
 00 11   word   SNAC subtype
 00 00   word   SNAC flags
 xx xx xx xx   dword   SNAC request-id
 xx xx xx xx   dword   Client idle time value

Example SNAC dump with flap header: 
                               
 2A 02 E5 65 00 12 00 01  00 1F 00 00 82 E8 D1 D1 *..e............
 00 00 00 3C                                      ....
